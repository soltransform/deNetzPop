About this map

Each cell on the map is a populated 100 m patch from the GHS-POP 2030 raster. Cells light up when their distance to the nearest open Tesla Supercharger is within the chosen radius.

Controls

← → adjust the distance.
Click a country on the map, or use the dropdown.
Smooth blends coverage at low zoom; Exact shows raw cells.

Caveats

Great-circle distance is from the cell center to a charger point. Road distance is computed on the OSM driving network; cells outside computed regions fall back to great-circle. Country borders use Natural Earth Admin 0; some include overseas territories. About 76 M people don't fall inside any country polygon.
